  translation.

  5. STREAM_SEGMENT_COUNT and KAFKA_TOPIC_PLAYBACK_EVENTS will be added to
  docker-compose.yml during the Generate step with the same defaults (safe, no
  behavioral change if omitted from compose).
  6. application/vnd.apple.mpegurl is used as the HLS manifest Content-Type.
  7. Auth Service's RSA public key is already written to the jwt-keys volume
  before the streaming service starts (enforced by compose health-check
  dependency chain).
